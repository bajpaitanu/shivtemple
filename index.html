About the Temple

Shiv Family Temple is a sacred temple located in Bhumbhuwar, Korari Kalan, Unnao, Uttar Pradesh. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and the divine Shiv Parivar including Maa Parvati, Lord Ganesha, Lord Kartikeya, Nandi Maharaj, and Lord Hanuman.
The temple is a center of faith and devotion where devotees visit daily to seek blessings, peace, and spiritual positivity. The calm and divine atmosphere of the temple makes it a peaceful place for prayer and meditation.
Special celebrations are organized during Mahashivratri, Shravan Maas, and Pradosh Vrat, attracting devotees from nearby villages and surrounding areas.

“ॐ नमः शिवाय”

Divine Atmosphere

The temple welcomes devotees every day for darshan, prayer, and spiritual peace. Its serene environment inspires faith, devotion, and positivity among visitors.

Daily Rituals

Morning Aarti

Everyday at 6:00 AM - Welcome the new day with devotion through Morning Aarti and Abhishekam to Lord Shiva.

Abhishekam

Daily Abhishekam with water, milk, honey and ghee performed with Vedic chants at 7:00 AM and 6:00 PM.

Evening Aarti

Join us at 7:00 PM for a soulful Evening Aarti followed by bhajans and prasad distribution.
