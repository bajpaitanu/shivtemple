About the Temple

Shiv Temple is a sacred place dedicated to Lord Shiva. Built in traditional Indian architecture, it stands as a symbol of peace, devotion, and spirituality.

Located in the heart of Unnao, the temple welcomes devotees for daily rituals, spiritual programs, and major festivals like Maha Shivratri.

"A place of divine worship and spiritual connection. Visit us and experience the serenity and blessings of Lord Shiva."

Daily Rituals

Morning Aarti

Everyday at 6:00 AM - Welcome the new day with devotion through Morning Aarti and Abhishekam to Lord Shiva.

Abhishekam

Daily Abhishekam with water, milk, honey and ghee performed with Vedic chants at 7:00 AM and 6:00 PM.

Evening Aarti

Join us at 7:00 PM for a soulful Evening Aarti followed by bhajans and prasad distribution.

Upcoming Events

Maha Shivratri

Join us on Maha Shivratri for an all-night vigil, chanting, and special Abhishekam from 8 PM till 5 AM.

Monthly Rudrabhishek

Special Rudrabhishek is performed every Pradosh Vrat. Devotees can participate by prior registration.
